Privacy Policy

Your data security is our priority

What We Collect

Job Tracker stores job application data locally in your browser using Chrome's local storage. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All information remains exclusively on your device.

How We Use Data

All job information is saved locally and only accessible to you through your browser. The extension processes your data solely to provide tracking functionality with no external servers or third-party services involved.

Data Security

Your job application data is protected by Chrome's built-in security features. Since all data remains on your device, it's not vulnerable to server breaches. You can clear your data anytime through Chrome's extension settings.

Contact Us

Have questions about our privacy practices? Reach out via LinkedIn. We're committed to transparency and will respond promptly to any inquiries.

Job Tracker is designed with your privacy in mind.
