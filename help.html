Your Job Tracking Companion

Never lose track of your job applications again! This extension keeps everything organized, so you're always prepared when opportunity calls. Say goodbye to lost emails and forgotten applications—stay in control of your job hunt!

Key Features

Job Application Management

Save and track job details easily. Alerts notify you of missing fields. View jobs in the latest-first order, update their status, and delete them with confirmation.

Notes Management

Add, edit, and delete notes in the job details view. Notes include timestamps and persist even after refreshing.

Job Data Management

Jobs are stored in Chrome's local storage for easy access. Changes update instantly, and deleted jobs are removed permanently.

How To Use Job Tracker

  1. Click the extension icon to open the job tracker.
  2. Enter job details and click "Save Job" to add it to your list.
  3. Click on a saved job to view details and add notes.
  4. Update the job status to keep track of your progress.

About The Creator

Hi, I'm Pearl, a Junior Front-End Developer and Certified Software Tester. After months of job searching with no clear plan, I found myself overwhelmed—losing track of applications, forgetting follow-ups, and struggling to stay organized. I knew there had to be a better way.

That's when I decided to build Job Tracker—a simple yet powerful tool to help job seekers keep everything in one place. With this extension, I can log job applications, update statuses, and track progress with ease. My goal? To finally check off some of those applications as "Hired!" and help others do the same.

I hope this tool makes your job search more structured, less stressful, and ultimately, more successful.
